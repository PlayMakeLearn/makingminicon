STEAM and Making MiniCon

An all-day gathering for maker educators & STEAM leaders

July 8, 2026
8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Lunch included
Location
Oregon Public Library
200 N Alpine Pkwy
Oregon, WI 53575

The STEAM and Making MiniCon is an all-day pre-conference at Play Make Learn 2026. Experienced and novice maker educators, STEM/STEAM educators and leaders from schools, libraries, museums and other settings will come together to learn about creating and supporting maker-based learning experiences. We'll test out maker activities, share ideas and discuss broader issues that connect making with our organizations, schools and/or communities. Participants will leave with ideas, inspirations and a small network of co-conspirators to initiate or continue their maker educational work.

You do not need to attend for the full Play Make Learn Conference to attend the MiniCon.
